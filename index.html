About

Hi, I'm Anupama — physician, coach, and your biggest advocate.

I know what it's like to pour everything into your career, to chase the next achievement, and to still feel like it's not quite enough. As a physician and certified life coach, I've worked with high-achieving professionals who have accomplished incredible things — and yet are quietly exhausted, self-critical, and unfulfilled.

My work is about helping you close that gap: between who you are and who you want to feel like. Between your external success and your internal peace. It's not about working harder — it's about finally working with yourself, not against yourself.

Together, we'll address the perfectionism, self-doubt, and fear of failure that are holding you back, and build the lasting confidence and balance you've always deserved.

Do you relate?

You're not alone in feeling this way.

High-achieving professionals often share the same invisible struggles. If any of these sound familiar, you're exactly who I work with.

01

The weight of perfectionism

No matter how much you accomplish, it never feels like enough. You're always raising the bar — and running yourself ragged to reach it.

02

Constant self-doubt

Deep inside, a voice questions whether you really deserve your success — and you're terrified others might figure that out too.

03

Burnout & exhaustion

You're overworked, overstretched, and running on empty. The drive that got you here is now leaving you depleted.

04

Fear of failure

Playing it safe feels necessary — because failing would mean something is fundamentally wrong with you, not just the outcome.

05

Difficulty setting limits

Saying no feels impossible. You overcommit, overdeliver, and over-explain — and quietly resent it all.

06

Searching for more

You want balance, peace, and the freedom to simply be yourself — not a version of yourself performing for everyone else's approval.
