What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make sites work, or work more efficiently, and to provide information to site owners.

Cookies on this Site

This Site is a simple static website and does not set its own tracking or advertising cookies. However, some third-party resources we load may set cookies or make network requests from your browser:

  • Google Fonts — fonts are loaded from Google's servers, which means your browser contacts Google when a page loads.
  • Formspree — used to process the contact form; it may set cookies or use similar technologies when you submit a message.
  • GitHub Pages — our hosting provider, which serves the Site's files.

If analytics are added to the Site in the future, this policy will be updated to describe any cookies they use.

How to control cookies

Most browsers let you view, manage, block, and delete cookies through their settings. Disabling cookies may affect how some third-party features (such as the contact form) behave. To learn more, visit your browser's help pages or allaboutcookies.org.
