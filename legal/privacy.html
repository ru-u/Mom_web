This Privacy Policy explains how Limelight & Leaf (Anupama Mohanram, MD) ("we," "us") handles information when you visit this website ("the Site").

How we use your information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information.

Third-party services

We rely on a small number of third parties to run the Site. Each has its own privacy practices:

  • Formspree — processes contact form submissions and delivers them to us by email.

  • Google Fonts — serves the typefaces used on the Site; your browser requests these files from Google.

Data retention

We keep contact form messages for as long as needed to respond to and maintain a record of our correspondence, after which they may be deleted.

Your choices

You may request that we delete the personal information you have sent us by emailing anupama@anupamamd.com. You can also choose not to submit the contact form and instead reach us directly by email.

Children's privacy

The Site is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above reflects the most recent revision.
